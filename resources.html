GSoC - All you need to know ft. Akshat Khare

Ever wondered what's Google Summer of Code (GSoC)? Well, DevClub IITD is here to help you with everything you need to know about GSoC. Google Summer of Code is a program to promote open-source development. It is organized by Google every summer for the last decade. It runs for 12 weeks from mid-May to mid-August. Students contribute to open-source repositories and get paid by Google in return. It's a great way to spend summer vacation
